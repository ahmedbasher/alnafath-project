Al-NAFATH Co. Ltd.

Al-Nafath Co. Ltd.
For Thermal, Environmental Systems Services & General Contracting is an Iraqi company established in year 2003 and considered one of the specialized companies in Iraq currently working among AGMEST group of companies whom have ISO 9001/2010 certificate.

Al-Nafath Employees
The company has well qualified staff of consultants, engineers and supporting staff that have very good experience gained from training outside Iraq with different specialties that covers the wide spectrum of the company activities in marketing and engineering services to our clients. This includes consultancy work, professional advice, decision making, systems requirements and options, marketing and sales, installation and commissioning and a solid program of after sale services.
